A refreshed system for Ataya Harmony

v1 patterns retuned with a tighter type scale, a perceptual neutral ramp, semantic surface tokens, and accessible focus rings. Brand anchors (the deep blue, the cyan accent, the teal up-state) are preserved so existing screens still feel like Ataya — just sharper.

Dialogs

Used for focused decisions and forms. The pattern preserves Ataya's right-side drawer for long forms (see PLAN.md), but adopts the centered confirm dialog for short interactions.

Tabs

Two flavors: an underline pattern for page-level switching (see Navigation), and a pill segmented control for inline filters.

Alerts & toasts

Inline alerts live with the content; toasts are transient and stack bottom-right. Both share the four intents tied to status colors.
